About Us

Elite Family Medicine provides high-quality patient care through offering several services in and out of office. We take pride in our commitment on serving the community and offering a wide range of medical services that are needed in the heart of Phoenix.

Learn More

Who are we?.

Elite Family Medicine is a high qualified group of supporting medical staff and dedicated providers. Our desire is to keep patients healthy by preventing diseases and illnesses while managing and treating existing ones. We are committed to putting our patients health first through treating, listening and educating our patients with high quality patient care.

In our best effort to improve quality patient care, we frequently document patients communications. We aim to set the standard of excellence in our community by providing the patients and their families, with confidence that show we are dedicated and committed of delivering responsive, professional and caring services to each and every patient, as a part of of a successful plan of care by ensuring that patients medical needs are addressed in a timely manner. If you have further questions or need any other assistance, please call us at (602) 296-5540. Our medical staff members will be more than glad to provide friendly assistance. Once again, we wish you a warm welcome to Arizona's First Choice Medical Services and we look forward to providing all of your health care needs.
